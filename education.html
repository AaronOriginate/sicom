The Challenge

Multi-building campuses.
Single-point-of-failure networks.

Universities and colleges face a unique connectivity problem. Dozens of buildings spread across a campus, each needing high-bandwidth access — but most are stitched together with ageing copper, leased lines, and contracts that were signed a decade ago.

Multi-building sprawl — faculties, labs, residences and admin blocks all demanding bandwidth from a network not built for it
Term-time constraints — any disruption during teaching weeks is unacceptable, making upgrades difficult to schedule
Exponential demand — students streaming, research data transfers, IoT sensors and hybrid teaching all competing for the same pipe
Legacy limitations — legacy copper and managed leased lines that can't scale without expensive new contracts
What We Provide

Infrastructure you own.
Bandwidth you control.

SICOM designs and installs campus fibre networks tailored to the way universities actually work — not a generic product bolted on to an existing telecoms contract.

Dark fibre links between campus buildings — fibre you own outright, connecting every faculty, lab and residence
Structured cabling — Cat6A and fibre distribution within buildings, designed to support current and future loads
Managed WiFi networks — high-density campus WiFi built for lecture theatres, libraries and outdoor spaces
Private 5G networks — dedicated cellular coverage for research applications and campus-wide mobility
IoT for building management — sensor networks for energy monitoring, access control, HVAC and space utilisation

Key Outcomes

What dark fibre means
for your campus.

Take Control

Stop relying on carrier-managed services that limit what you can do. Own the physical infrastructure and decide how bandwidth is allocated across your campus.

Minimise Costs

Replace recurring lease payments with a single capital investment. Dark fibre typically delivers 30-50% lower total cost of ownership over 10 years compared to managed services.

Flex Bandwidth

Upgrade capacity by changing the equipment at each end, not by renegotiating contracts. Scale from 1Gbps to 100Gbps without touching the cable in the ground.

Own Your Network

A dark fibre network is a physical asset on your balance sheet with a 25+ year lifespan. No vendor lock-in, no surprise price rises, no contract renegotiation every 3 years.
