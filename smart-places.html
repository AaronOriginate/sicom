Case Study — Science Park Development

End-to-end smart infrastructure for a 12-building science park

A regional science park developer needed to attract high-value tenants in the life sciences and technology sectors. Working with SICOM from the pre-construction phase, we designed and delivered a fully integrated smart infrastructure platform across all 12 buildings — including Digital Twin planning, IoT sensor networks, building management integration, managed fibre connectivity, and WiredScore Platinum certification for every building on the park.

The result: the development achieved full occupancy 14 months ahead of schedule, with rental rates 18% above the original business plan. Tenants benefit from seamless managed connectivity with no individual carrier contracts, while the park operator monitors energy consumption, air quality and occupancy in real time across every building from a single dashboard.
